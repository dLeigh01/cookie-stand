About Us

My name is Pat, and one day while wandering through my beautiful home city of Seattle, I realized that I wanted to combine two of the things I love the most about this city. Our delicious salmon, and our fantastic cookies!
Now, you may be wondering, how well can those two things really fit together? You'd be surprised! Our Salmon Cookies are sweet coffe-time confections made into the cute shape of a salmon and instilled with just a hint of fishy goodness. It's like salt and chocolate, two very different things that complement each other when combined!
I invite all of you to come in and see for yourself, you won't be able to help coming back for more!
